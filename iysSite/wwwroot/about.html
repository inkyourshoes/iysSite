About the Brand

Yo!

Welcome to my first, self-created website! My name is Edward Raff and I am 35 years old, living in Virginia Beach.

I love to play basketball, workout, skate, snowboard, chill with my gf, and create art!

I have enjoyed a variety of jobs over the last decade, primarily teaching. However, while in college, I was always interested in tattooing. Fortunately, my artist, Rob, was a friend. I left his house with a new tattoo, and all the equipment needed to pursue the idea. Somehow, along that journey, I ended up making hundreds of customized pairs of shoes. I've designed shoes for newborns, students of mine, and even some of my favorite professional athletes!

I am so grateful for the opportunity to create art and connect with so many unique people. Thank you for visiting my site!
